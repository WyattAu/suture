available under [AGPL-3.0-or-later](LICENSE-AGPL). You can:

- Use Suture for any purpose, including commercial use
- Modify and redistribute under the same license
- Self-host the hub without any fees or restrictions

## Self-Hosted = Always Free

`suture-hub` is the self-hosted collaboration server. It is **always free**
under AGPL-3.0-or-later. No billing, no tier limits, no feature gates.

## FAQ

### Can I use Suture in my commercial product?
Yes, if you self-host. The AGPL-3.0 license requires you to provide
the source code of any modifications you make to Suture itself.

### Do I need a commercial license to self-host?
No. Self-hosting `suture-hub` is always free under AGPL-3.0.

### Can I use the platform without paying?
Yes, the free tier provides 100 merges/month, 100 MB storage, and 5 core
drivers. Upgrade when you need more.

### How do I get a commercial license?
Contact license@suture.dev or sign up at the hosted platform and
upgrade your plan.
